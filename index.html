About

I am a Software Developer originating from a small town in Austria, who recently graduated with a Masters degree in Computer Science from University College London (UCL), for which I received a First-Class Honours including a Dean's List award.

I am passionate about iOS, backend and web development, which has supported me during a wide range of projects over the last four years. To this end, I pride myself on producing maintainable code that reflects industry standards, while also actively contributing to the developer community on StackOverflow.
